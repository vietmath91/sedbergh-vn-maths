1. Simplify $2^3 \times 2^4$.
2. Simplify $5^6 \div 5^2$.
3. Simplify $(3^2)^4$.
4. Write $\frac{1}{2^3}$ as a power of 2.
5. Simplify $7^3 \times 7^{-1}$.

<details><summary><strong>Answers</strong></summary>

1. $2^7 = 128$  
2. $5^4 = 625$  
3. $3^8 = 6561$  
4. $2^{-3}$  
5. $7^2 = 49$

</details>
